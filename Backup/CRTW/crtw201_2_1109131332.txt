--[Logical Fallacies]--
   [Post Hoc]
      -Post Hoc, Ergo Propter Hoc
      -After This, Therefore also this
   [Hasty Generalizations]
      -make basis of not a lot of information
      -becauuse destiny 2 had 58% less sales than
        destiny 1, then it means it was worse. this
        doesnt include the 1.2 million concurrent players
   [Sweeping Generalization]
      -make too much out of too little
      -uses all, must, always, etc.
      -"All fast food is fattening
   [False Analogies]
      -reach a conclusion by comparing one thing to
        another
      -because these two things are true, why cant this
        be true as well
         -false analogy because it does not include the
           deeper reasons or some externalities
      -Forcing students to attend culteral events is
        like hearding cattle to slaughter
         -the students stampede into the event where
           they are systematically put to sleep by the
           program
   [Red Herrings]
      -too little revelence to the argument at hand
      -changing the question at issue
   [Either or arguments]
      -one or the other, no middle ground
      -this is itself a falacy
      -we go to point a or we dont go anywhere at all
   [slippery slope]
      -one thing will lead to another no matter what
      -little evidence to support it
   [Bandwagon]
   --
      -popular
   [Ad Hominem]
      -attacking the opponent
